About Me

Hello there. I’m Gabriel, a 37 year old guy from Buenos Aires, Argentina. I’m a Business Intelligence Analyst, freelance web developer, IT Engineering undergrad and husband.

This website is an escape of my ideas and projects. Through the years I setup a couple of websites but never stuck with them. This time I won’t send the site to oblivion as it’s being hosted in GitHub.

Expect some table top RPG, IOT and domotics/home automation, stuff as well as some BI and bits of things I may find useful for a broad spectrum of people. Expect me to publish in both English and Spanish, depending on the target demographic of the particular piece.

You may reach me @GabrielZorrilla. Thanks for stopping by ❤️!
